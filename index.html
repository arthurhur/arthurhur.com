Hi, it’s Arthur!

I’m mostly a creative director that specializes in filmmaking, design and animation. More recently, I enjoy programming and developing too.

For over ten years, I’ve worked with the talented team at Google Creative Lab, building and making things to make the world a better place. Some highlights include:

  • Launching Project Loon to deliver internet to the neediest areas of the world.
  • Redesigning the Google Logo into a living system that responds to users.
  • Organizing your life with the personalized Google Assistant.
  • Debuting Material Design, the design framework behind Android and Google.
  • Showcasing various VR Experiments for Chrome.
  • Demoing AI-generated conversations in real-time with Google Duplex.
  • Building the largest AR monument in history, Stonewall Forever, to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the Stonewall uprising.
  • Revealing the next generation of AI tools with Google LaMDA.
  • Creating compelling visuals for Hacking Google, a six-part documentary about the teams that keep us safe on the internet.
  • Bringing the search bar alive for an amitious campaign to show "New ways to search".

Before working with Google, I created countless campaigns for clients like Konami, Calvin Klein, Cartoon Network, Citroën, Legomania, Sim City, British Gas, and music videos for Beck, Iron & Wine, My Morning Jacket, J Dilla, and TV on the Radio.

And before that, I worked alongside some of the most iconic artists of our time, like Michel Gondry, Martin Scorsese and Laurie Anderson.

I have also built up a lot of other unique experiences over the years, from being a pop-up chef and magician, to founding and developing a startup to solve misinformation, to live-streaming as a hedge fund manager.

I am a firm believer that it is better to do than to direct, and better to show than to say.

Feel free to contact me to learn more or collaborate on something you're excited about!
