  • We were thrown into the deep end on this one, but learned so much along the way.

    And here's the director's cut. Because the concept and workflow were all being figured out in parallel, we viewed work in this style for a while.

    When we were first pitching ideas and working through concepts, we knew we needed to have the logo and the car. After we figured out how to wrap the Chrome logo around the Sphere and drive cars around it, we knew this was going to work.

    We also knew that we needed to convey speed. Just having the cars drive fast around the Sphere wasn't enough. Hence, light streaks!

    There were lots of dead ends and stumbles along the way, but it was fun to flesh through some ideas.

    In the end, McLaren and Formula 1 did not want anything that conveyed smoke or pollution, so we had to find a different solution.

    There were countless visual and technical details to hash out along the way.

    And this is how crazy it all looks when you unwrap everything onto a flat render.

    When this Chrome activation first launched, it was the only one of its kind. Every year since, we've seen many rip-offs of cars and streaks driving around the Sphere. So it goes. Just remember, you saw it here first!
